Here is a timeline for Dr Albert Einstein life

  • 1879 – Born in Ulm, Germany.
  • 1889 – Begins interest in science and mathematics.
  • 1895 – Moves to Switzerland for studies.
  • 1900 – Graduates from Zurich Polytechnic.
  • 1902 – Works at Swiss Patent Office, Bern.
  • 1905 – Publishes “Annus Mirabilis” papers (special relativity, E=mc², etc.).
  • 1905 – Publishes “Annus Mirabilis” papers (special relativity, E=mc², etc.).
  • 1915 – Completes General Theory of Relativity.
  • 1919 – Theory confirmed by solar eclipse; gains worldwide fame.
  • 1921 – Wins Nobel Prize in Physics.
  • 1933 – Moves to the U.S., fleeing Nazi Germany.
  • 1939 – Warns U.S. about atomic research (Einstein–Szilard letter).
  • 1945 – Advocates for peace after WWII.
  • 1955 – Dies in Princeton, New Jersey.
